ABOUT GOAL TRACKER

The app was created to help people with their daily needs. The average working class person
has suffiecient time to attend to the present needs he/she has on a daily basis and this makes other
non-physically present needs suffer. The goal tracker app was created in such a such a simple user
interface that any person learned enough to own a smart phone or generally, an electronic gadget can
use it with ease.
It possesses a sign in page that collects the user information and stores it so the user would be recognised
at a later date. it also has a dashboard where the user can see his/her details about the goals he/she has
created. The dashboard also has the option to create a new goal.
In order to achieve utmost satisfaction from the app, users are enjoined to check the app at least once
a day to ross-out tasks that they have completed or to enter new information based on the level of progress
that they have reached on the current task.
The app was created as a task given to a team of hardworking software engineers who are remote interns at
Hotels.NG. They pulled ideas together and came up with the most creative, user-friendly way to deliver the
to the users.
